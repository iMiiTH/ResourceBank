Resource Bank

The Resource Bank is the replacement for the CSCU's Exam Bank. It was completely rewritten to be a self-contained and self-hosted application on campus. It features web-based administration so anyone is able to easily upload, edit and modify content on the site.

All files on the site were uploaded by students or professors, and belong to their respective owners. If you see any content on this site that you do not with to be available for students, contact us at cscu@scs.ryerson.ca.

The site is hosted on the servers of the Department of Computer Science. A special thanks to Ivan Rubiales for his help.

For technical information about the project, see the Github page!

About the Developers

The Resource Bank is a collaborative effort by students, for students in Computer Science at Ryerson University. Development was led by Mitchell Mohorovich, with help from David Tenty and Frank Vumbaca.
